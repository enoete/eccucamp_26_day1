ECCB/Credit Bureau is the monetary authority and central bank for eight member countries in the Organisation of Eastern Caribbean States (OECS). As the regional body responsible for credit reporting infrastructure, their work touches every citizen who interacts with a bank, a utility, a hire purchase company, or a telecommunications provider. Their mandate is financial stability, financial literacy, and public trust in the credit system across the Eastern Caribbean.

Services Offered

About the Organisation

The Inland Revenue Division (IRD) is a division within the Ministry of Finance of Grenada. It is responsible for the administration of Inland Revenue laws and the collection of a wide range of taxes and licences — including personal income tax, corporate tax, general consumption tax, stamp duty, and other taxes. IRD also handles vehicle, telecommunications, and other licence registrations, business registration for tax purposes, property valuation and assessment, and property transfer tax. Their challenge: engaging a public that only pays attention during peak tax season.
